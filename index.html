Hi, I'm Laura.

I am an Academic Foundation Doctor and NHS Clinical Entreprenuer currently working in Cardiff, having graduated from Imperial College London School of Medicine with an intercalated BSc in Cardiovascular Sciences (First Class Honours). I am the creator of medical education app Spin The Bleep and Co-Founder of the Imperial College School of Medicine Coding Society.

I am passionate about medical innovation and a competent programmer in Python, R and Matlab, with additional skills in cross-platform app development (Flutter/Dart). I am always seeking new academic opportunities and medical technology projects to get involved in alongside my junior doctor jobs.
